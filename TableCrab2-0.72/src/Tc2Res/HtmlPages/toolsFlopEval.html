Flop evaluator
Flopzilla like tool to evaluate hand ranges against flops.

you may enter a hand range by either clicking hand types in the grid or by using the slider / spin or by entering a hand range into the editbox. in case you entered a hand range in the editbox, hitting return will set the grid to all complete hand types in the range entered, that is, single cards are not being reflected.

next you can either select a flop in the grid to the right and hit Evaluate flop or hit Evaluate random to evaluate the range against all possible flops.
  • evaluated is always the content of the editbox. slider, spinbox and the grid are mere tools to fill the editbox in some predefined ways.


the meters to the bottom show the result of an evaluation, that is the percentages a hand range hits on a certain or all possible flops:
  • StraightFlush: percent straight flushes
  • Quads: percent quads
  • FullHouse: percent full houses (excluding flops that are a set)
  • Flush: percent flushes
  • Sets: percent sets
  • TwoPair: percent two pairs (excluding paired flops)
  • OverPair: percent overpairs (including flops that are a set)
  • TopPair: percent top pairs (excluding overpairs)
  • Pair: percent pair (excluding overpairs and top pairs)
  • FlushDraws: percent flush draws
  • StraightDraws: percent straight draws


the editbox recognizes the following (comma separated) patterns:
  • JhQh: a single two card combination
  • TT: a single pair
  • TT+: all pairs X and above
  • TT-22: all pairs X to Y
  • KTs: a suited two card combination
  • KTs+: all suited two card combinations XY and above
  • KTs-K7s: all suited two card combination XY to XZ
  • KTo: an offsuit two card combination
  • KTo+: all offsuit two card combinations XY and above
  • KTo-K7o: all offsuit two card combination XY to XZ
  • KT: a two card combination suited and offsuit
  • KT+: all suited and offsuit two card combinations XY and above
  • KT-K7: all suited and offsuit two card combination XY to XZ
  • random: all hands
  • suited: all suited hands
  • offuit: all ofsuit hands
  • pairs: all pairs
  • broadways: all brodway hands
