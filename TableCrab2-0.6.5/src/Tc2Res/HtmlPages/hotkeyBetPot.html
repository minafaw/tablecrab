Hotkey Bet Pot
puts current pot amount multiplied by Multiplier into the bet box. use this hotkey for 1/1 1/2 (...) pot sized bets. a multiplier of 0.5 for example is a good choice for a hotkey to make half pot sized bets.

to use this hotkey you have to tell TableCrab where the pot is. every template on the Setup tab has two points for this: PotTopLeft and PotBottomRigth. doubleclick on the locations on the screenshot. add some space to the left and right as shown below just to make shure the region is still covered in case PokerStars needs to resize the pot to make room for bigger bets. 


    PotTopLeft
    \

            |              |
            | POT $15,500  |
            |______________|
                                x
                                 \
                          PotBottomRight
  • this hotkey may or may not work depending on your table theme. you have to find out yourself. in the background an ocr engine tries its best to make sense out of the region you pointed it to. watch the messages on the status bar to get an idea if things are working for you or not.

    as a rule of thumb: a highly contrasing background is better than low contrasting one. a plain as background is better than a rough.
