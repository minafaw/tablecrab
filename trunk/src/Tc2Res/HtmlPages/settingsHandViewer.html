Settings Hand Viewer
here you can fine tune what is displayed in the hand viewer tab

Side bar position
here you may adjust the position of the side bar in the hand viewer

Deck style
here you may adjust the style of the deck used in the hand viewer. not that changes take effect on the next hand loaded.

Max Player Name
here you may adjust the maximum lenght of player names. if a player name is longer than this number of chars it will get truncated. set to -1 to not truncate at all.

Floating point to integer
if checked, TableCrab will try its best automatically convert floating point bet amounts and stack sizes to integer values. maybe helpful at the micros.

Prefix | Action | Postfix
you may adjust prefixes and postixes for actions. for example a bet is displayed as "b100". set prefix to "bets(" and postfix to ")" and the action will be displayed as "bets(100)".

Restore Default
restores default prefixes and postfixes for all actions

  • Note: changes will take effekt as soon as the next hand is grabbed
