Settings Card Protector
settings of the card protector

Show n startup
check to show the card protector whenever TableCrab is started

Background color
adjusts the background color of the card protector. you may Reset to default at any time.

Background image
sets an image as background of the card protector. you may Reset to default at any time.

Auto toggle
if checked, the card protector is hidden when hitting the hotkey and automaticaly shown again after Auto toggle timeout seconds.

Auto toggle timeout
sets the timeout (in seconds) after that the card protector is automatically shown again.
