Hotkey Fold
whenever you hit this key TableCrab will fold the hand for you. that is - it either hits the "Fold" button or checks the "Fold" checkbox.
  • currently checkbox fold AND button fold have to be in the same location to make this hotkey work. this may or may not get fixed in future releases to support layouts that do not meet this condition. for now just make shure both points as specified in the template are within the bounds of button AND checkbox.
